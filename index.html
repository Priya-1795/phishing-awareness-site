What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of cyberattack where attackers trick users into revealing personal information such as passwords or financial details through fake emails, websites, or messages.

Common Phishing Techniques

  • Email phishing with malicious links or attachments
  • SMS phishing (Smishing)
  • Voice phishing (Vishing)
  • Fake login pages

How to Stay Safe

  • Don't click suspicious links
  • Verify the sender's email address
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • Report phishing attempts to your IT team or service provider
